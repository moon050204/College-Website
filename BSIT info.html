Course Highlight

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program provides students with a strong foundation in computer systems, programming, networking, and software development. Students gain hands-on experience through lab work and internships, working with cutting-edge technologies and industry-standard tools. The program covers areas such as database management, cybersecurity, web development, and systems analysis, preparing graduates for roles in various IT fields. With a focus on problem-solving, critical thinking, and innovation, BSIT graduates are equipped to meet the growing demands of the technology industry and pursue careers in tech companies, IT consulting, and software development.
