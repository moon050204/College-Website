Course Highlight

The Bachelor of Nursing program builds a strong foundation in healthcare principles, patient care, and ethics. Through clinical placements, students gain hands-on experience in real settings, exploring specialties like pediatric, geriatric, mental health, and surgical nursing. The curriculum emphasizes evidence-based practice and leadership skills, preparing graduates to make informed decisions, lead care teams, and promote community health. This program shapes versatile, compassionate professionals ready for diverse roles in hospitals, clinics, and public health.
