Course Highlight

The Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM) program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of hospitality principles, including hotel and resort management, food and beverage operations, tourism, and event planning. Students develop essential skills in customer service, leadership, and operations management through hands-on training, internships, and real-world projects. The program also emphasizes critical thinking, effective communication, and sustainable practices in the hospitality industry. Graduates are well-prepared to pursue careers in various hospitality sectors, including hotel management, event coordination, food service, and tourism operations, and excel in a dynamic and global industry.
