Course Highlight

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business principles, including management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship. Students develop essential skills in leadership, decision-making, and strategic planning through case studies, internships, and hands-on projects. The program also emphasizes critical thinking, effective communication, and ethical business practices. Graduates are well-prepared to pursue careers in various business sectors, including management, human resources, marketing, and financial analysis, and can lead organizations to success in a competitive global market.
