Course Highlight

The Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM) program prepares students for a dynamic career in the tourism and hospitality industry. It covers key areas such as tourism planning, event management, hospitality management, and sustainable tourism practices. Students gain hands-on experience through internships in travel agencies, hotels, and event management companies. The program emphasizes customer service, business skills, and cultural awareness, equipping graduates with the tools needed to manage tourism operations and contribute to the growth of the global tourism industry. Graduates are well-prepared for roles in travel agencies, tour operations, hotel management, and event coordination.
