St. Mary’s College of Baliuag Inc. are a trusted institution with a rich history as the first and oldest Catholic school in Baliuag, committed to quality education that fosters Catholic values. We stand out as the only PAASCU Level III accredited school in Baliuag, meeting high standards and continuously improving to enhance the learning experience for our students. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification further affirms our commitment to quality assurance, achieved through our partnership with TUV Rheinland Philippines.

At SMCB, we are pioneers in adopting multiple intelligences and personalized learning styles into our programs, allowing students to maximize their unique abilities. We embrace innovation, particularly in adapting to the evolving "new normal" of education, through our QTIME (Quality Transformative Ignacian Marian Education) framework. This approach integrates technology and flexibility, providing students with an adaptable and comprehensive learning experience.

SMCB is a place where the differences between "less," "better," and "best" are discovered. Now is the time to experience the transformation and feel the change—where faith, excellence, and service are always reign. Together, let us embrace the path of progress meant for you. Join us today, take your place, and let your spirit and mind soar. Because at SMCB, you know the difference.

VISION

We, the Ignacian Marian community of St. Mary's College of Baliuag, witness the compassionate love of God and advocate quality transformative education towards the fullnes of life in Jesus Christ.

MISSION

We commit ourselves to:

1. Grow in discernment and interior freedom and be prophetic witness of faith, hope, and love in today's world;


2. Continuously form Ignacian Marian leaders who witness to faith, Excellence, and service in varied socio-cultural settings;


3. Constantly pursue innovatice programs, approaches, and educational and technological strategies to develop global citizens;


4. Build up resources and capabilities and create new paradigms to address social harmony toward a dignified life; and


5. Expand our educational thrust for the poor.

CORE VALUES

Faith

Strong faith in God, prophetic witness to gospel values nationalism, justice and communion.

Excellence

Discipline, integrity, self-reliance, competence and resourcefulness

Service

Preferential love for the poor, stweardship, humility, charity and courage
