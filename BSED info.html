Course Highlight

The Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) program equips students with the knowledge and skills to teach at the secondary level. It covers core subjects such as curriculum development, educational psychology, and assessment, along with specialized training in the chosen teaching field. Students benefit from practical teaching experience through internships in schools, where they apply classroom theories in real teaching environments. The program emphasizes effective communication, classroom management, and the development of a positive learning environment. Graduates are prepared to inspire and educate the next generation of learners in various schools.
